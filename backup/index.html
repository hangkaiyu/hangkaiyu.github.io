Bio

I am a Ph.D student at the Centre for Autonomous Systems and the Computer Vision and Active Perception Lab at KTH Royal Institute of Technology , Stockholm, Sweden. My current research interests include robotic precision/fingertip grasping, object-hand joint representation, adaptable grasping and grasp manifold learning etc. I am generally very interested in robotics, machine learning, AI and computer vision. I was previously involved in the EU project TOMSY and am now working on the ERC project FlexBot .
